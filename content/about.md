## Brand story

### EN (live draft)

Maison Novatio was born from a simple observation: the Canadian market is sorely lacking in fast, durable, and affordable housing solutions — particularly in remote regions where traditional construction costs reach 3× the national average.

Founded by Renaud Lacoursiére Théroux, a licensed electrician and serial entrepreneur based in Nicolet, Quebec, Maison Novatio set out to change this reality. By sourcing directly from the world's leading manufacturers of modular and prefabricated structures — aviation-grade aluminum, extreme-cold insulation, integrated smart systems — the company offers a modern, certifiable, turnkey alternative to conventional construction.

In two years, Maison Novatio has submitted more than 120 proposals across Quebec, sold 2 demo units, and is currently in active negotiation for a major government project in Nunavik — 14 Indigenous communities, federal funding up to CAD $750,000 per project. The digital platform under development will soon allow clients to virtually tour models in 360°, configure their finishes, and order directly online — from anywhere, at any time.

**Maison Novatio. No hesitation.**
